<font size="30">debug</font> (system *)
These commands are used for debugging purposes.

<font size="22">setcurrentmode <modeNumber></font>
Sets the current mode or prefix to `modeNumber`. Mode numbers can be found at https://ooflet.github.io/docs/quickstart/prefix
<font color="rgb(150,150,150)">debug setcurrentmode 1</font>
